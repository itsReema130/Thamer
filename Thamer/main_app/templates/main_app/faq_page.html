THAMER is a complete dynamic website through which you can enter as a company owner or local supplier. Local content in Saudi Arabia is defined as the total economic expenditure within the country, achieved through the involvement of Saudi elements across workforce, goods, services, production, and technical assets.

THAMER is designed for all companies located in the kingdom and outside the kingdom that have Iktva score and local content score. This includes companies operating in the energy and local sector, as well as companies in other sectors that are required to meet local content and Iktva requirements.

THAMER provides a number of benefits for both companies and local suppliers, including:
  • Increased visibility: THAMER's catalog makes it easy for companies to find and qualify local suppliers, and for suppliers to showcase their products and services to potential customers.
  • Improved performance: THAMER's advisory services can help companies improve their local content and Iktva performance, increase their chances of winning government and Aramco contracts, and save money.
  • Reduced risk: THAMER's centralized platform helps companies manage their local content and Iktva compliance, reducing the risk of fines and penalties.
